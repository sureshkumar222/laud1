Kazi Nazru Islam

The national poet of Bangaladesh

Rebel poet

kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recgnized as aBidrohi Kobi (rebel poet) around the country.Nazrul is afamous poet,musician and philosopher of Bangladesh.The famous poet Nazrul's writing deal with the revolution of the period of the British givernment.His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh.He has raised his vice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awesome writer

Nazrul's writings expolored themes sucg as love,freedom,humanity, and revolution.He opposed all forms of bigority and fundamentalism,including religious,caste-based and gender-based.Throughout hid career,Nazrul wrote short stories,novels,and essays but is best known for his songs and poems.He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals.Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs (many recorded on HMV and gramophone records),collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.
